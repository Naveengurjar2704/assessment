About the App

VegIt is a mobile application built for residents of 3-4 nearby societies in Ghaziabad to order fresh vegetables and milk directly from local farmers. We aim to simplify daily grocery shopping with doorstep delivery, trial options, and subscription services.

Core Features

  • OTP-based user authentication via phone number

Why We Need a Payment Gateway

Currently, we support Cash on Delivery. To scale and provide a better experience, especially for milk subscriptions and prepaid delivery slots, we are seeking integration with UPI-based payment solutions like PhonePe, Google Pay, etc.

Target Audience

The app is designed for a limited user base of 3-4 societies, making it ideal for small-scale, hyperlocal logistics and easy order management.

Status

Note: The app is currently under development with key modules like authentication, product listing, cart, subscription, and order placement already completed.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. VegIt collects only essential information such as your phone number and delivery address. We do not share user data with third parties and ensure secure handling of all personal information.

Return & Refund Policy

Customers can request returns or cancellations before delivery. If unsatisfied with the quality of vegetables or milk, we offer full refunds or replacement. Returns must be initiated within the same day of delivery.

Payment Intent

All online payments made via the gateway will be directed toward prepaid milk subscriptions or vegetable orders. UPI integration will support faster, secure, and contactless payments, reducing the reliance on cash handling.
